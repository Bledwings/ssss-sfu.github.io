About us

Who are we? We are the Software Systems Student Society (SSSS) that represents the Software Systems program here in Surrey. This student society represents the interests and welfare of its members to the Software System Program and to Simon Fraser University, Surrey, Canada. The SSSS office is located in the fourth galleria of the SFU Surrey campus. You are more than welcome to drop by!

Our goal is to:

- Foster and grow a sense of community both on and off campus

- Host a variety of educational and fun events including guest speakers, seminars, workshops, and social events

- Provide services of benefit to students in Software Systems, as well as to the campus in general

- Maintain representation on behalf of Software Systems students at the SFSS Forum, and provide student input to the program director
